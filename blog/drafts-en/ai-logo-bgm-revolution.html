AI-Generated Logos + BGM: The New Revolution in Brand Building

Travis Chen 2026-04-21 7 min read

The traditional brand-building process goes like this: find a designer, communicate brand philosophy, wait 1-2 weeks for a logo draft, go through 3-5 rounds of revisions, and finally spend NT$20,000-50,000 and a full month just to get a logo. As for brand music? Most SMEs don't even consider it — because it's too expensive.

But AI is changing all of that. In HEY!BOSS 銀月數位顧問's website building process, both logo design and BGM background music are generated by AI. The quality rivals manual design, the timeline shrinks from a month to minutes, and costs are dramatically reduced.

1. AI Logo Generation: Design, Not Assembly

Many people's first reaction to "AI-generated logos" is skepticism. This concern is understandable — a few years ago, AI could only produce crude icon collages. But in 2026, AI image generation technology is completely different.

What AI logo generation can do today:

  • Understand brand personality — Input "upscale Japanese cuisine" versus "affordable street food," and AI generates completely different logo styles
  • Color scheme matching — AI automatically selects primary, secondary, and background colors based on industry characteristics and brand personality
  • Multiple versions output — Generates color, white background, and dark background versions in one go for different use cases
  • Vector processing — Output in SVG format, scalable without quality loss, usable for both print and digital

2. AI BGM Generation: Every Brand Deserves Its Own Music

Background music has always been a "luxury" in brand building. Hiring a musician to compose a brand soundtrack costs anywhere from NT$30,000 to NT$300,000. Licensing stock music runs several thousand to tens of thousands per year. The result: 90% of brand websites have no background music.

AI music generation changes this dynamic. The AI music generation technology used by HEY!BOSS produces unique BGM based on brand characteristics:

  • Style specification — From classical, jazz to electronic, Lo-fi — AI precisely matches the brand's desired music style
  • Emotional tone — Warm, energetic, professional, luxurious... different emotional tones can all be expressed
  • Length control — 30 seconds to 3 minutes, designed to match website browsing duration
  • Copyright-free — AI-generated music copyright belongs to the brand, no additional licensing fees needed

3. Quality Comparison: AI vs. Manual

This is what everyone cares about most. Here's an objective comparison:

Logo Design Comparison

  • Top-tier designer: Uniqueness 5/5, Quality 5/5, Cost NT$100,000-500,000, Time 1-3 months
  • Mid-level designer: Uniqueness 4/5, Quality 4/5, Cost NT$20,000-100,000, Time 2-4 weeks
  • AI-generated: Uniqueness 4/5, Quality 4/5, Cost near zero, Time minutes
  • Template-based: Uniqueness 2/5, Quality 3/5, Cost free to a few thousand, Time minutes

AI-generated logos have already reached quality levels close to mid-level designers, and far surpass templates in uniqueness. For SMEs with limited budgets, this is the highest value-for-money option.

BGM Comparison

  • Professional musician: Quality 5/5, Cost NT$30,000-300,000, Time 2-4 weeks
  • Music library licensing: Quality 3/5, Cost thousands to tens of thousands/year, Time instant (but not unique)
  • AI-generated: Quality 4/5, Cost near zero, Time minutes

4. HEY!BOSS's AI Brand Building Process

In HEY!BOSS's website building service, AI brand building is the very first step:

  1. Brand data collection — Industry category, brand name, brand personality, target audience
  2. AI logo generation — 3-5 options generated based on brand data, client selects or mixes
  3. AI color scheme — Complete color palette including primary, secondary, gradient, and background colors
  4. AI BGM generation — Unique background music generated based on brand personality
  5. Brand integration — Logo, colors, and music integrated into the brand website

The entire process takes less than an hour. And every step allows human intervention for adjustments — AI is the tool, but final decision-making remains in the brand owner's hands.

6. Where Are the Limits of AI Brand Design?

Honestly, AI brand design has its limitations:

  • Extremely custom requirements — If the brand needs very intricate hand-drawn styles or special craft effects, manual design is still better
  • Brand strategy planning — AI can handle visual design, but brand positioning and market strategy still require human wisdom
  • Cultural sensitivity — Use of certain cultural symbols needs human oversight

That's why HEY!BOSS's process is an "AI generation + human review" hybrid model, not purely automated.
